## 30-Day Low-Touch Roadmap (minimizing owner involvement)

### Week 1 (Days 1–7): Foundation + Quick Revenue Lifts
1. Identify top 15 listings by sales/reviews.
2. Create 3 bundle offers from existing assets.
3. Add commercial license add-on to top 10 listings.
4. Build cross-sell map (each listing → 3 related listings).
5. Set up standardized delivery PDF (download, edit, support links).

**Owner time:** ~2–3 hours approval only.

### Week 2 (Days 8–14): Automation Infrastructure
1. Deploy support automation:
   - Etsy saved replies for top 20 FAQs
   - FAQ PDF + “start here” onboarding
2. Build SEO automation sheet:
   - Keyword cluster columns
   - Title/tag generation prompts
   - Weekly A/B logging template
3. Implement upsell assets:
   - Thank-you page/PDF with next-step offers
   - Coupon for premium tier

**Owner time:** ~1–2 hours review.

### Week 3 (Days 15–21): Expansion SKUs
1. Launch Lite/Premium split on top 5 bestsellers.
2. Produce first B2B pack for strongest niche.
3. Release 1 seasonal variant collection (5–10 SKUs).
4. Localize top 5 listings (ES first, then FR/DE queue).

**Owner time:** ~2 hours brand/quality check.

### Week 4 (Days 22–30): Scale + Optimize
1. Review conversion data:
   - Bundle attach rate
   - Upsell take rate
   - AOV and message volume
2. Double down:
   - Expand winning bundle into additional niches
   - Promote best-performing license tier
3. Prepare recurring “vault” offer landing + content calendar.
4. Set monthly autopilot cadence:
   - 4 new variants/month
   - 1 new bundle/month
   - 1 localization batch/month
   - Weekly SEO refresh batch

**Owner time:** ~2 hours KPI decision meeting.
