# Realtor Lead Gen Script Kit - Asset Pack

Use these as copy/paste templates. Replace bracketed fields.

## A) First-Contact DMs (15)
1. Hey [Name], quick question—are you still thinking about moving in [Area] this year?
2. Hi [Name], I just saw a home in [Area] that fits what most buyers are asking for lately. Want the details?
3. Hey [Name], if I sent you 3 homes that match [budget/area], would that be helpful?
4. Hi [Name], are you open to seeing what homes in [Area] are actually selling for right now?
5. Hey [Name], I help [first-time buyers/sellers] in [Area]. Happy to send a simple game plan if you want one.
6. Quick one: are you looking to buy before [season/date]?
7. If you had the right property in [Area], would you move in the next [3-6] months?
8. Hey [Name], I made a short list of homes with [feature]. Want me to send it?
9. Curious—are you waiting for rates, or still actively browsing?
10. I can send a quick estimate of what your home could list for right now. Want it?
11. Are you considering upsizing or downsizing this year?
12. If I could help you avoid common offer mistakes, would you want a 5-minute checklist?
13. Have you been to any open houses lately, or still researching online?
14. I’ve got a few off-market style opportunities from my network. Want me to keep you in the loop?
15. If the numbers made sense, would you sell in the next 6 months?

## B) SMS Outreach (10)
1. Hey [Name], it’s [Agent]. Still interested in homes in [Area]?
2. Quick check—want me to text you 3 listings under [$X]?
3. Are you free this week for a quick call about your move timeline?
4. I can send current comps for your neighborhood if helpful.
5. Would Saturday or Sunday be better for showings?
6. I found a property that matches your top criteria. Want the link?
7. If I sent a monthly market update, would you want it?
8. Want a no-pressure home value estimate this week?
9. Are you still considering selling this year?
10. I’ve got an opening at [time]. Want me to reserve it for you?

## C) Follow-Up Sequence
### Day 1
Hey [Name], just circling back—want me to send options in [Area] based on [budget/bed/bath]?

### Day 3
No rush at all. If now isn’t the right time, I can check back next month.

### Day 7
I put together a quick snapshot of [Area] pricing trends. Want me to send it?

### Day 14
If timing changed, no problem. If you want to restart later, I’m happy to help.

### 30-Day Reactivation
Hey [Name], market shifted in [Area] this month—want a fresh update before you decide next steps?

## D) Objection Handling (8)
### "We’re not ready"
Totally fair. Most people take a little time. Want a simple prep checklist so you’re ready when timing feels right?

### "We already have an agent"
Understood. If anything changes, I’m happy to be a backup resource. Want my free neighborhood market updates?

### "Just send details"
Absolutely. I’ll send highlights now. If useful, we can do a 10-minute call to tailor options.

### "Rates are too high"
That makes sense. I can show how similar buyers are structuring offers right now to keep payments manageable—want that breakdown?

### "We’re just browsing"
Great place to start. Want me to send a low-pressure shortlist weekly so you can track the market without overwhelm?

### "We need to think"
Of course. What’s the biggest question you still need answered? I can usually clear that up quickly.

### "Not interested"
No worries, thanks for the reply. If market updates would ever help, I can send occasional insights.

### "Too busy"
I get it. Want a super short summary by text so you can review when you have 2 minutes?

## E) Open House + Appointment Follow-Up (7)
1. Thanks for stopping by [Address]. What stood out most to you?
2. Based on your feedback, I can send 3 similar homes. Want me to?
3. Any concerns about layout, location, or pricing I can help evaluate?
4. Would you like a quick offer strategy if you decide to move forward?
5. Appreciate your time today—next best step is a quick financing check. Want an intro?
6. If this one isn’t it, I can line up 2 better-fit options this week.
7. Thanks for touring. Are you leaning yes, no, or maybe?

## F) Referral Ask Templates (5)
1. If you know anyone planning to buy/sell in [Area], I’d be grateful for an intro.
2. I grow mostly by referrals—if someone comes to mind, I’ll take great care of them.
3. Know anyone who needs a reliable agent this season?
4. If you have a friend thinking of moving, I can send them a free market snapshot.
5. Thanks again—referrals are always appreciated.

## G) Quick Call Script (Buyer)
- "What areas are your top 2 choices?"
- "Target monthly payment range?"
- "Must-have vs nice-to-have features?"
- "Ideal move date?"
- "Would you like me to send a tailored shortlist today?"

## H) Quick Call Script (Seller)
- "What’s your ideal timeline to list?"
- "Have you done any recent upgrades?"
- "Do you need to buy before/after selling?"
- "Would a quick pricing strategy review help?"

## I) CRM Cadence Cheat Sheet
- New lead: Day 0, Day 1, Day 3, Day 7, Day 14, Day 30
- Warm lead: 1-2 touches/week
- Cold lead: monthly reactivation
- Referral lead: respond within 5 minutes when possible
