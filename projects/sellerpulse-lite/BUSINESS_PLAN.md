# SellerPulse Lite - Business Plan (v1)

## Positioning

**"Turn Etsy listing stats into clear weekly actions in 60 seconds."**

Target customer: Etsy and digital-template sellers doing $0-$10k/month who don't know what to optimize next.

## Offer Ladder

1. **Free Tool (Lead Magnet)**
   - SellerPulse Lite web tool
   - Captures email for updates

2. **Paid Product ($19-$39 one-time)**
   - SellerPulse Pro spreadsheet + templates
   - Includes KPI dashboard + action library + pricing test matrix

3. **Recurring Service ($49-$149/mo)**
   - Monthly listing optimization recommendations
   - Optional listing copy rewrites + experiment queue

## Fast GTM

- Publish Lite tool demo in Etsy seller communities + Reddit + X threads
- Create a downloadable PDF lead magnet: "7 Etsy KPI rules that grow revenue"
- Add CTA in all your Etsy product thank-you files:
  - "Get a free listing growth diagnosis"

## Revenue Targets (realistic)

- 30 days:

  - 20 paid product sales @ $29 = $580
- 90 days:
  - 500 free users
  - 70 paid sales @ $29 = $2,030
  - 20 subscribers @ $79 = $1,580 MRR
