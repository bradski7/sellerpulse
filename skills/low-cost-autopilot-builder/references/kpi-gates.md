# KPI Gates (Scale / Improve / Kill)

## Universal gates (first 14-30 days)

- **Acquisition sanity:** At least 100 qualified visits/users in period
- **Activation signal:** At least 10 meaningful actions (favorites, signups, trials, carts)
- **Conversion floor:** 1.5%+ purchase/checkout conversion (or equivalent lead conversion)
- **Economic sanity:** Positive unit economics trajectory before paid scale

## Etsy-style gates

- CTR target: 1.5%+
- CVR target: 2.0%-3.0%+
- Favorites-to-order trend improving week-over-week
- AOV increasing via bundles/cross-sell

### Action logic
- Low CTR + low views -> fix thumbnails/titles/tags first
- Good views + low CVR -> fix offer clarity/price/FAQ/proof
- Good CVR + low volume -> expand distribution or small paid tests

## Software micro-SaaS gates

- Landing conversion target: 15%+ to email/waitlist for early stage
- Trial/demo-to-paid target: 5%-10%+
- Churn signals tracked by week 4+

## Budget release rules

Move from $0 to paid tests only if:
1) conversion floor is met,
2) offer is clear and repeatable,
3) measurement loop is in place.

Suggested paid test cap (early): $100-$300 total.

## Stop-loss rules

Stop paid test if:
- CAC exceeds gross profit per user/order by >30%, or
- 2 optimization cycles fail to improve conversion materially.
